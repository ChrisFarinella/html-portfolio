The Best Movies According to Chris

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Pulp Fiction

This is probably Quentin Tarantino's best movie to date. It is pretty good.

Barbarian

This is the best horror movie that I've seen in a while. Keeps you guessing at every turn.

Dragon Ball Super: Broly

This is by far the best Dragon Ball movie that has been made so far. The animation is top notch.
